---
title: AllergyIntolerance Resource
description: Patient allergy and intolerance records
---

The AllergyIntolerance resource records risks of harmful or undesirable reactions when exposed to substances. All records must reference a valid Patient.

## Endpoints

### Create AllergyIntolerance

<Note>
  Requires **admin** or **practitioner** role.
</Note>

```http
POST /api/fhir/AllergyIntolerance
Authorization: Bearer {token}
Content-Type: application/json
```

<ParamField body="resourceType" type="string" required>
  Must be `"AllergyIntolerance"`
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="clinicalStatus" type="object">
  `active`, `inactive`, `resolved`
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="verificationStatus" type="object">
  `unconfirmed`, `confirmed`, `refuted`, `entered-in-error`
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="type" type="string">
  `allergy` or `intolerance`
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="category" type="array">
  `food`, `medication`, `environment`, `biologic`
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="criticality" type="string">
  `low`, `high`, `unable-to-assess`
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="code" type="object" required>
  Substance that causes the allergy
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="patient" type="object" required>
  Reference to Patient: `{"reference": "Patient/{id}"}`
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="onsetDateTime" type="string">
  When allergy/intolerance was identified
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="reaction" type="array">
  Adverse reaction events
</ParamField>

## Criticality Levels

<Warning>
  **High criticality** allergies should be prominently displayed in the patient chart to prevent adverse reactions.
</Warning>

| Level | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| `low` | Low risk of harmful outcome |
| `high` | High risk of serious or life-threatening reaction |
| `unable-to-assess` | Unable to assess risk |
